2. Description of Service

Pray: Christian Prayer Tracker is a personal prayer management application that allows you to:

  • Create and organize prayer items with custom icons and colors
  • Track prayer history and check-ins with timestamps
  • Set prayer alarms and reminders with customizable durations
  • Sync your data privately across all your Apple devices via iCloud (optional)
  • Integrate with your device's calendar (optional)
  • Use widgets and Live Activities to access prayer timers on your home screen, lock screen, and Dynamic Island
  • View prayer statistics, streaks, and activity history
  • Receive helpful tips and guidance through in-app suggestions

The App is designed to help you maintain a consistent prayer life and track your spiritual journey.
